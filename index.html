Title: The Sidekick Problem

Characters:
  • Bolt: A flashy superhero who moves at lightning speed.
  • Zap: His overconfident but clumsy sidekick.

    • Another night, another crime to stop! You ready, Zap?

    Always! I even brought snacks this time.

    Snacks? We’re supposed to blend in, not crunch in!

    But fighting crime on an empty stomach is bad for focus.

    That’s our cue! Move out!

    Wait, can I finish my—
    —chips… never mind.

    I… got here… just in time… right?

    Yep. Just in time to help me carry him to the police.

    Hero work is exhausting.

    Maybe next time skip the snacks.

    Maybe next time you slow down!

    You are Always the problem
